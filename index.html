What is our Aim

Our aim is to deliver integrated solutions that empower individuals and involve their support networks, fostering a compassionate and informed environment.

AI Solution for Mental Health

AI innovations for scalable, to create an eco- friendly environment for overall well being . By addressing individual needs, we aim to alleviate the societal burden of mental health issues.

Enhancing Accessibility for Disabilities

By harnessing the potential of artificial intelligence and mobile technology, we aspire to transform the learning, communication, and social dynamics for Deaf, Mute and Blind communities.

AI in Managing Eating Disorders

With the help of AI we will be using a health promotion approach, focusing on building self-esteem, positive body image, and a balanced approach to nutrition and physical activity.

Our Solution

Ensuring that these AI solutions are seamlessly integrated into everyday life, providing continuous, real-time assistance and fostering independence, while also being sensitive to the unique needs and circumstances of each individual.

Our Vision

The brief on the process and methods by which Bionic Care will solve the problems.

Chatbots and Virtual Therapists:

Using NLP models like GPT-4 for Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to simulate therapeutic conversations and provide real-time support.

Predictive Analytics:

Machine learning models like decision trees and neural networks for early detection and personalized treatment plans.

Emotion Recognition:

Deep learning models (CNNs,RNNs) for analyzing facial expressions and voice intonations to detect emotions and provide empathetic responses.

Braille Learning and Usage:

Real-time Braille translation using CNNs (Convolutional Neural Network) and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology integrated into smartphones and tablets.

Enhanced Sensory Perception:

Computer vision models like YOLO for object detection and apps like Seeing AI for providing audio descriptions.

Sign Language Recognition:

AI models using CNNs and RNNs for translating sign language into text or speech.Examples include Googleâ€™s MediaPipe Hands.

Assistive Communication Devices:

Real-time captioning using speech-to-sign language models like DeepSpeech, CNN and RNN.

Personalized Treatment :

Machine learning models (SVMs,Random Forests) for tailored treatment.

Real-Time Monitoring:

Wearable gadgets with sensors for tracking food intake, physical activity, and physiological signals. Using CNNs and LSTMs (Long Short Term Memory)for data analysis.

Emotional Support:

AI chatbots using NLP for delivering CBT techniques and offering 24/7 support.

Crisis Management:

Detecting health risks and alerting support networks through wearable sensors and anomaly detection algorithms.
